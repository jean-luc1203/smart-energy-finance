## 📊 Overview

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jean-luc1203/smart-energy-finance/main/smart-energy-finance/docs/images/economie.png" width="800"/>

Smart Energy Finance is an advanced Home Assistant add-on designed to:

* 💰 Calculate real energy costs
* ⚡ Track solar production value
* 🔋 Evaluate battery profitability
* 📈 Analyze savings over time

---

## ⚠️ Important recommendation

👉 It is **strongly recommended** to use:

* ✅ Energy sensors (kWh, daily counters)
* ❌ NOT power sensors (W)

Why:

* More accurate calculations
* No drift over time
* Reliable daily / monthly / yearly stats
